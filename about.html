ABOUT HA-SET INITIATIVES

Recently asked questions

People Are Frequently Asking Some Questions

What is Haset Initiative?

Haset Initiative is a women entrepreneurship initiative that aims to empower and support aspiring women entrepreneurs in starting and growing their own businesses. We provide resources, mentorship, and a supportive community to help women overcome challenges and succeed in their entrepreneurial journey.

Who can participate in Haset Initiative?

Haset Initiative is open to all women who have a passion for entrepreneurship and a desire to start their own business ventures. Whether you have a specific business idea or are looking for inspiration, we welcome women from diverse backgrounds and industries.

What kind of support does Haset Initiative provide?

Haset Initiative offers a range of support services to women entrepreneurs. These include mentorship programs, networking opportunities, educational workshops and webinars, access to funding resources, and guidance on business development and strategy. We believe in providing comprehensive support to help women entrepreneurs thrive.

How can I get involved with Haset Initiative?

To get involved with Haset Initiative, you can start by visiting our website and signing up for membership. We regularly update our members with upcoming events, workshops, and opportunities. You can also join our online community to connect with other like-minded women entrepreneurs and learn from their experiences.

How can I become a mentor with Haset Initiative?

If you are an experienced entrepreneur or business professional who wants to give back and support women in their entrepreneurial journey, we welcome you to become a mentor with Haset Initiative. Please reach out to us through our website or contact our mentorship program coordinator for more information on how to get involved via +251923577911.

How can Haset Initiative help me in growing my existing business?

Whether you are just starting out or have an existing business, Haset Initiative can provide valuable support to help you grow. Our resources and programs cover various aspects of business development, such as marketing, scaling, financial management, and leadership. We also offer networking opportunities that can help you connect with potential partners, clients, and investors.
